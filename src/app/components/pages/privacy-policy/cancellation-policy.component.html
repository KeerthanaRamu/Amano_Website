Purpose of this policy

This policy sets out the circumstance that a student or customer is entitled to a refund and the procedure a student or customer must follow to withdraw from a Scheduled Driving Curriculum Lesson to get a refund.

Refund Policy

The Driving School offer of a place is contingent upon payment of the Application and Administration Fee.

Refunds

The Application and Administration Fee is not refundable. Except in cases of provider default, refunds will only be made in the following circumstances: a) Where you cancel BEFORE the Scheduled Driving Curriculum Lesson Start Date If written notice is received not less than 5 days prior to the Lesson Start Date the Driving School will, within 28 days, refund the selected driving package fees less the application and administration fees. b) Where you withdraw from the Scheduled Driving Curriculum Lesson AFTER the Start Date If written notice is received not less than 5 days prior to the Lesson Start Date the Driving School will, within 28 days, refund the selected driving package fees less the application and administration fees, Attended Lesson Fees, Withdrawal Fees and Cancellation Fees

  • If you defer starting a Driving Lesson and then cancel the Driving Lesson, the original Scheduled Driving Start Date before the deferment(s), will be used as the Driving Lesson Start Date to determine whether a refund is to be made.
  • No refund will be made where the Driving School cancels, suspends or terminate your enrolment as a result of your
    • conduct that subject to disciplinary actions
  • If you are over 18 years old, the refund will be paid directly to you or in accordance with your written directions.
  • If you are under 18 years old, the refund may be paid to you but only with the authority or consent of your parent or legal guardian.
    • The Driving School may; Request further information or evidence to confirm that you are the person entitled to receive the fund or give a direction to pay the refund
  • Pay the refund to a bank account by direct credit to the locally registered bank but only if you provide the evidence that the banks exist
  • All refunds will be made in Malaysian currency only
  • You agree that the Driving School may change the policy at anytime and the policy to be used to determine whether you receive a refund will be the policy applicable at the time you give notice of cancellation to withdraw.

Provider default

In cases of Provider (Driving School) default that, where the Driving School cancels or stop providing your lesson for any reason, the Driving School will, within 28 days, refund on a pro rata basis, the Driving Lesson Package Fees for the days the lesson conducted, unless: i. you withdraw from a Scheduled Lesson before it is cancelled; or ii. you accept an offer in an alternative course arranged by the Driving School

Payment Terms

The Course fees shown on SEKOLAH MEMANDU AMANO Website are inclusive of GST/SST where applicable and must be paid upfront in order to take on a license driving course. These fees might change at SEKOLAH MEMANDU AMANO convenience without prior notice and do not represent a formal quotation for future date. A candidate allowed making his/her payment progressively. The management will keep the candidate informed on the mode of payment and has the rights to vary the fee / payment as and when necessary. How to withdraw from Scheduled Driving Lesson

Written notification to refund@smamano.my with the subject (Request for Cancellation by Your IC Number) The Driving School will process your email request and assess whether you are entitled to a refund based on our policy. The Driving School may ask further information or documentary evidence

Tujuan dasar polisi

Polisi ini menetapkan keadaan bahawa pelajar atau pelanggan berhak mendapat bayaran balik dan prosedur yang mesti diikuti oleh pelajar atau pelanggan untuk menarik diri daripada Pelajaran Kurikulum Memandu Berjadual untuk mendapatkan bayaran balik.

Polisi Bayaran Balik

Sekolah Memandu menawar syarat yang bergantung kepada pembayaran Yuran Permohonan dan Pentadbiran.Purpose of this policy

Polisi Pembayaran Balik

Yuran Permohonan dan Pentadbiran tidak akan dikembalikan. Kecuali dalam kes lalai pembekal, bayaran balik hanya akan dibuat dalam keadaan berikut: i. Di mana anda membatalkan SEBELUM Tarikh Mula Pelajaran Kurikulum Memandu Berjadual Jika notis bertulis diterima tidak kurang daripada 5 hari sebelum Tarikh Mula Pelajaran, Sekolah Memandu akan, dalam masa 28 hari, membayar balik yuran pakej pemanduan yang dipilih ditolak yuran permohonan dan pentadbiran. ii. Di mana anda menarik diri daripada Pelajaran Kurikulum Memandu Berjadual SELEPAS Tarikh Mula Jika notis bertulis diterima tidak kurang daripada 5 hari sebelum Tarikh Mula Pelajaran, Sekolah Memandu akan, dalam tempoh 28 hari, membayar balik yuran pakej pemanduan yang dipilih ditolak yuran permohonan dan pentadbiran, Yuran Pelajaran yang Dihadiri, Yuran Pengeluaran dan Yuran Pembatalan

  • Jika anda menangguhkan memulakan Pelajaran Memandu dan kemudian membatalkan Pelajaran Memandu, Tarikh Mula Memandu Berjadual asal sebelum penangguhan, akan digunakan sebagai Tarikh Mula Pelajaran Memandu untuk menentukan sama ada bayaran balik perlu dibuat.
  • Tiada bayaran balik akan dibuat apabila Sekolah Memandu membatalkan, menggantung atau menamatkan pendaftaran anda akibat kelakuan anda yang tertakluk kepada tindakan tatatertib
  • Jika anda berumur lebih daripada 18 tahun, bayaran balik akan dibayar terus kepada anda atau mengikut arahan bertulis anda..
  • Jika anda berumur di bawah 18 tahun, bayaran balik mungkin dibayar kepada anda tetapi hanya dengan kebenaran atau persetujuan ibu bapa atau penjaga sah anda.
  • Sekolah Memandu boleh; Minta maklumat atau bukti lanjut untuk mengesahkan bahawa anda adalah orang yang berhak menerima dana atau memberi arahan untuk membayar bayaran balik Bayar bayaran balik ke akaun bank melalui kredit terus kepada bank berdaftar tempatan tetapi hanya jika anda memberikan bukti bahawa bank itu wujud
  • Semua bayaran balik akan dibuat dalam mata wang Malaysia sahaja
  • Anda bersetuju bahawa Sekolah Memandu boleh menukar polisi pada bila-bila masa dan polisi yang akan digunakan untuk menentukan sama ada anda menerima bayaran balik akan menjadi polisi yang terpakai pada masa anda memberikan notis pembatalan untuk menarik diri.

Lalai Pembekal

Dalam kes keingkaran Penyedia (Sekolah Memandu), iaitu, di mana Sekolah Memandu membatalkan atau berhenti menyediakan pelajaran anda atas sebarang sebab, Sekolah Memandu akan, dalam masa 28 hari, membayar balik secara prorata, Yuran Pakej Pelajaran Memandu untuk hari pelajaran dijalankan melainkan; anda menarik diri daripada Pelajaran Berjadual sebelum ia dibatalkan; atau anda menerima tawaran dalam kursus alternatif yang diatur oleh Sekolah Memandu

Bagaimana untuk menarik diri daripada Pelajaran Memandu Berjadual

Pemberitahuan bertulis kepada refund@smamano.my dengan subjek (Permintaan Pembatalan dengan Nombor Kad Pengenalan Anda) Sekolah Memandu akan memproses permintaan e-mel anda dan menilai sama ada anda layak menerima bayaran balik berdasarkan dasar kami. Sekolah Memandu boleh meminta maklumat lanjut atau bukti dokumentari
